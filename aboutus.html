About Us


Our Branches


Nugegoda

Grocies Nugegoda offers a convenient shopping experience in the bustling town of Nugegoda. Our branch is well-stocked with fresh produce, groceries, and household essentials. Whether you're looking for local favorites or international brands, we have it all to meet your needs.

Kandy

Welcome to Grocies Kandy, your one-stop destination for quality groceries and more. Situated in the heart of Kandy, our store offers a wide range of products to cater to your daily needs. From fresh fruits and vegetables to pantry staples and household items, we provide everything you need for a hassle-free shopping experience.

Colombo

Grocies Colombo is your go-to supermarket in the vibrant city of Colombo. Conveniently located on DEF Street, our store offers a diverse selection of products to satisfy your grocery needs. Explore our aisles filled with fresh produce, quality meats, delicious snacks, and much more. Shop with us and experience unmatched convenience and value.
